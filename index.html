Welcome to My San Francisco!

A city of diverse culture, beautiful sunsets, and iconic landmarks.

The Mission District

I love the vibrant Mexican culture here, from street food to art and music. It’s like a piece of Mexico in the heart of San Francisco.

Chinatown

San Francisco's Chinatown is one of the largest and oldest in the U.S. It’s full of rich history, markets, and beautiful architecture.

Pier 39 & Crab House

One of my favorite spots! I love the seafood and garlic fries at Crab House. It’s a must-visit for anyone who loves good food and a lively atmosphere.

Union Square

Union Square is the perfect place for shopping. Stores like Nordstrom, Neiman Marcus, and Saks Fifth Avenue make it a shopper's paradise!

Alcatraz & Golden Gate Bridge

Alcatraz prison and the Golden Gate Bridge are iconic! The views from Vista Point or Golden Gate View Point offer a breathtaking panorama of the bridge and city.

Sunsets at the Pier

The sunsets in San Francisco are amazing! Watching the sky change colors from the Pier is one of my favorite things. I just live in the moment and take it all in.

Exploring San Francisco one day at a time, soaking up the culture, views, and flavors!
